# 🌻 uXu: The Archive Without Limits

> **No gatekeepers. No standards. No requirements—just culture.**

uXu is a decentralized archive platform. We believe culture belongs to the people who lived it, and that any attempt to "standardize" or "govern" how that culture is presented is an obstacle to preservation.

## Purpose
uXu is a public archive collection. The repository exists to host community-contributed materials—live performances, lectures, cultural events, or anything else you choose to archive. 

## How to Contribute
uXu belongs to the community. There are **no requirements** for your archive entries, provided they are **legal and safe**.

### Your Archive, Your Way
We do not believe in templates, schemas, or forced design standards. Every archive entry is a standalone example of how a contributor wants to present their data.

1. **Fork the Repository:** Create your own copy of uXu.
2. **Add Your Archive:** Create a new folder under `archives/` for your collection.
3. **Build What You Want:** You are responsible for your own `data.json`, `index.html`, or any other format you choose to use. 
4. **Submit a Pull Request:** We only review submissions to confirm they adhere to basic safety and legality standards. Your aesthetic, your folder structure, and your technical approach are entirely up to you.

### Our Only Rules
- **Legal:** You must have the right to share the content.
- **Safe:** Content must not violate basic safety guidelines (e.g., harassment, dangerous content, etc.).

*That is all. There are no other rules. No contribution is too strange, too simple, or too experimental.*
